About Us

Women in Computing (WiC) is an all inclusive organization that provides an interdisciplinary networking forum for students, faculty, staff and alumni involved in the field of computing at Rochester Institute of Technology through professional, leadership, mentorship, and technical development opportunities. WiC at RIT also encourages the advancement of women and all gender minorities in computing through community outreach.
